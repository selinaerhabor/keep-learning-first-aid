Frequently Asked Questions

KLFA stands for Keep Learning First Aid. We are UK based providers of First Aid learning and training material. Since we were founded in 2016, we are now offering a range of products from CPR Manikins to First Aid Manual books for all ages in order to equip people with the skills and confidence needed to provide first aid in emergency situations.
We do have a small collection of First Aid Kits available for purchase from our online store. However, in the case of an emergency, please call 999 immediately. If you live outside the UK, in the case of an emergency, please call your emergency services immediately.
